HARBOUR | A timeline of events

Year 510

Q4

  • Fishing vessels operating from the island of Toma begin experiencing attacks.
  • New Olympian State initiates a naval blockade to the western region of Emerald Sea in response to the attacks.
  • Caripatha enters into negotiations with the Republic of Ladum regarding potential joint military operations on the island of Baradun.
  • Sugar plantations in the Kingdom of Danewa are hit by a new fungal disease, leading to a shortage in global supply.
  • The People's Republic of Venera sells military equipment to the Dorsan Republic.
  • A ceasefire between the Satruji Republic and the Kingdom of Tyrella was broken in a border skirmish before another ceasefire was established shortly after the conclusion of the fighting.
  • The Callistan Republic announces plans to begin construction of the world's first microchip factory.
  • Taham, a member nation of HETU, accusses Harberra of economic sabotage via restriction of maritime trade.
