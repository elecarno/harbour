HARBOUR | A timeline of events

Year 510

Q4

  • Fishing vessels operating from the island of Toma begin experiencing attacks.
  • New Olympian State initiates a naval blockade to the western region of Emerald Sea in response to the attacks.
  • Caripatha enters into negotiations with the Republic of Ladum regarding potential joint military operations on the island of Baradun.
  • Sugar plantations in the Kingdom of Danewa are hit by a new fungal disease, leading to a shortage in global supply.
  • The People's Republic of Venera sells military equipment to the Dorsan Republic.
  • A ceasefire between the Satruji Republic and the Kingdom of Tyrella was broken in a border skirmish before another ceasefire was established shortly after the conclusion of the fighting.
  • The Callistan Republic announces plans to begin construction of the world's first microchip factory.
  • Taham, a member nation of HETU, accusses Harberra of economic sabotage via restriction of maritime trade.

Year 511

Q1

  • Tyrellan-aligned rebels in the eastern-most province of the Satruji Republic initiate an uprising and allow forces of the Kingdom of Tyrella to occupy the province.
  • The Mercurian Assyrian Confederation forms the Cimmeran Economic Cooperative with the nations of Aclura, the Atlai Kingdom, Simmea, and the Republic of Haclesh.
  • The Mercurian Assyrian Confederation forms an exclusive trade deal with the Callistan Republic for Germanium in exchange for Callistan support in developing MAC mining infrastructure.
  • The Mercurian Assyrian Confederation celebrates 75 of confederacy with a military parade in the nation's capital of Solari
  • HETU signs a free trade agreement with the Empire of the Ascension of Sol for Cerium and Neodymium.
  • The Empire of the Ascension of Sol signs a trade agreement with the Callistan Republic for Cobalt in exchange for military command over three Callistan Air Force squadrons.
  • The Republic of Nova Alba declares its intentions to close its borders and begin operating a closed and controlled economy.
  • Arrolia develops the fishing industry in the Gale Islands
  • Arollia and the Republic of Yawen sign a deal to standardise and develop rail infrastructure within the two countries to allow for open trade between either nations ports in both the Emerald Sea and the Sirennan Ocean.
  • Caripatha and the Republic of Ladum begin joint military exercises from the island of Baradun and the construction of a joint military facility on the island.
  • Rossara repots a major buildup of forces of the Dorsan Republic along the Dorsan-Rossaran border.
  • HETU concludes that Havirra was enacting policies that were detrimental to the union as a whole and enacts a full re-evaluation of the union's trade legislation.
  • Naval vessels of Ignara Olympia are spotted patrolling the eastern coasts of Landha.
  • The Republic of Lusnia begins military expansions and invests in microchip production.
  • The Serdovan Collective attempts to gather intel on occurrences past the Olympian blockade using long-distance optical and radar observation equipment.

Q2

  • The People's Republic of Venera joins the Satruji-Tyrellan war on the side of the Satruji Republic.
  • Independent weapons brokers are caught selling to the Kingdom of Tyrella from the Empire of the Ascension of Sol.
  • The council of the CEC announces major economic plans to "rebuild" the region of Cimmera following its long history of conflict.
  • The Mercurian Assyrian Confederation sets a new rail speed record with their experimental train, the XHS-03 "Arrowhead".
  • The Republic of Haclesh signs a trade deal to exchange reduced rates on small arms for fighter jets from the Mercurian Assyrian Confederation.
  • The Millar State signs a trade agreement regrading increased flow of automobiles and raw materials with the CEC.
  • Arrolia encounters and engages into brief fighting with an unidentified submarine off of the coast of the island of Goliath.
  • Arrolia sends further investment into the development of the Gale Islands.
  • The Gael Kingdom shuts down seafaring traffic through the Koval Straight.

Q3

  • The United Kingdom of the Vislands invades and occupies the island of Toma.
  • The Echoan Defence Pact is formed by the Ryland Republic, Sinitria, the Caron Kingdom, Mafri, the Republic of Vemya, and Theziland.
  • The People's Republic of Venera and the Satruji Republic near the completion of an encirclement of the southern half of the Kingdom of Tyrella.
  • A rare earth mine collapses in the Risanov Kingdom, dropping global supply of Cerium and Neodymium by 15%.
  • The Republican Conservative Party maintains power following elections in the Mercurian Assyrian Confederation.
  • High speed rail construction begins in the Mercurian Assyrian Confederation.
  • Arrolia expands it's rail infrastructure project to West Uglaria.

Q4

  • The Mercurian Assyrian Confederation provides support to mine collapse rescue efforts in the Risanov Kingdom
  • The Cimmeran Economic Cooperative signs a trade agreement for lower tariffs with the Republic of Lichona
